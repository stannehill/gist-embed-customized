Embed Gists and Preserve Line Numbering in Extracted Chunks

This page provides examples of gists being used in their entirety (the standard gist embed behavior) and being used in chunks. Other gist enbed script do this but they tend to make a single ajax request each time a gist is referenced (resulting in, say 5 requests of the same gist) and/or they don't offer to preserve line numbering (meaning that the chunk you are referencing is renumbered from line 1). This script preserves line numbering (great for referencing specific portions of the main gist) and only makes one ajax request per gist. Enjoy!
